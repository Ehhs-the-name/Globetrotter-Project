A Bit About Osaka

This website will show you a couple of interesting places you can see in Osaka if you choose to visit.

Osaka is one of the three major cities in Japan, with over 2.6 million residents. It's also the third safest city in the world which makes it a great place for a vacation.

Traditionally considered Japan's economic hub, it still holds most of that energy with the countless tourist destinations residing inside.

Central Osaka is home to many attractions and landmarks, however, it's good to keep in mind that it's broken down into its uptown and downtown areas: Kita (キタ, "north") and Minami (ミナミ, "south"). Well-known districts in Minami include Namba and Shinsaibashi shopping areas, the Dōtonbori canal entertainment area, Nipponbashi Den Den Town, as well as arts and fashion culture-oriented areas such as Amerikamura and Horie.
